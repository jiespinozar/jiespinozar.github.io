Juan Ignacio Espinoza Retamal

About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Astrophysics at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (PUC), currently doing an internship at the University of Amsterdam (UvA). I completed my undergraduate studies at PUC in Chile. My main research interests are on exoplanets, from demographics to architectures considering observational and theoretical points of view.
